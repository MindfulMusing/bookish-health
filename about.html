Frequently Asked Questions

Is it safe to use generic Cialis without a prescription?

While some individuals may use generic Cialis without consulting a doctor, it is strongly recommended to seek professional advice to avoid potential health risks and ensure the medication is suitable for your condition.

What are the side effects of generic Cialis?

Common side effects include headaches, indigestion, back pain, and flushing. More serious side effects may occur and require immediate medical attention, underscoring the need for medical consultation.

Can I purchase generic Cialis online?

Yes, generic Cialis is available through various online pharmacies. However, it is crucial to verify the pharmacy's credibility and consult a healthcare provider before making a purchase.
